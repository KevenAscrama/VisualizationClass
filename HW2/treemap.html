Treemap of Phone Brands' Market Share

Description:
This tree map shows the brands' market share. One brand contains many versions of their phones. The larger the amount it gets, more bigger the rectangular will be.
Meanwhile, the different color means the different year the phone was released. The newer it is, the bluer it will be. (Tip: The excat data is all made up.) Left-click can zoom in and right-click can zoom out.
